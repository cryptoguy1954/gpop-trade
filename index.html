✗ Do NOT Enter
Price pokes above resistance but closes back below. This is a fakeout — traps buyers. Wait.
✓ Entry Signal
2 consecutive closes above resistance, each higher than the last. Now confirmed — enter on open of candle 3 or at pullback.
